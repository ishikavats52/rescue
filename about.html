ABOUT RESCUECONNECT

Welcome to RescueConnect, the ultimate hub for rescue teams everywhere.

Our mission?

To make sure help is always at hand when disaster strikes. Whether you're a rescue agency looking to connect with peers or someone in need trying to find the closest help, we've got you covered. With our interactive map, you can see all the rescue teams near you , and our filters let you find the right team for your specific needs. Teams can also use RescueConnect to chat, collaborate, and make sure every crisis is responded to swiftly and efficiently. Safety and privacy are our top priorities. Dive in and let's make the world a safer place,together!
