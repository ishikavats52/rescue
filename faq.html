FAQ's

What is AppConnect?

AppConnect is a platform designed to connect rescue agencies, allowing them to collaborate, communicate in real-time, and share resources and information.

How do I register my rescue agency on AppConnect?

Go to our registration page and fill out the required details. submission, our team will verify the authenticity of your agency before granting full access. Is my agency’s data secure on AppConnect? Absolutely! We prioritize user data security and have implemented stringent measures to ensure data is stored securely and not shared with unauthorized third parties.

How does the broadcast feature work?

The broadcast feature allows agencies to send out a wide-reaching message for immediate assistance or resources. It’s designed for urgent situations requiring a coordinated response.

Can I communicate directly with other agencies?

our platform offers real-time communication tools, including chat, video, and calling features, allowing agencies to connect directly.

How can I update my agency’s profile and information?

After logging in, navigate to your agency’s profile page where you can edit and update all relevant details.

If I notice inaccurate information or misuse on the platform?

We encourage users to report any discrepancies or misuse. Please contact our support team with details, and we will address the concern promptly.

Is AppConnect available worldwide?

Yes, AppConnect is designed for rescue agencies from all around the world. Our goal is to foster global collaboration and coordination.

Are there any costs associated with using AppConnect?

As of now, AppConnect offers its core features for free to all verified rescue agencies. For premium features or extended services, please refer to our pricing page.

If i forgot my password, How can I regain access to my account?

On the login page, click on the "Forgot Password" link and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Can I collaborate with international agencies through AppConnect?

Absolutely! AppConnect is designed to bridge the gap between local and international rescue agencies, fostering a global community of support.
