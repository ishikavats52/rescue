RECENT NEWS

A sudden collapse of a multi-story building in downtown Baghpat today could have turned tragic if not for the rapid response of emergency teams. Within moments of the incident, rescue operations were underway, successfully extracting all trapped residents. Hospitals report most victims sustained minor injuries, but several remain in critical condition. The community's resilience and unity have been a silver lining, with many rallying to provide immediate aid. City officials are now probing into the cause, suspecting structural faults. This incident underscores the vital importance of building safety and timely inspections.



Today, the Tamil Nadu Express, 12622 derailed outside Chennai, resulting in overturned carriages and trapped passengers. Emergency teams reacted swiftly, ensuring all aboard were extracted. Local hospitals have been attending to the injured, with several in critical condition. The cause of the accident is currently under probe. The commendable speed and efficiency of the rescue operations, combined with the community's immediate support, became a shining light in the face of adversity. The incident emphasizes the crucial need for rigorous rail safety checks.



West Bengal Wildlife teams, aided by local communities, triumphed today in Sundarban, rescuing a cheetah e.g., "trapped leopard cub" from a dangerous predicament. Amid challenging conditions, the team showcased exemplary skill and dedication, ensuring the animal's safety. Now under expert care, the creature is on the path to recovery. This collaborative effort stands as a testament to the region's commitment to protecting its native wildlife, highlighting the profound bond between humans and the natural world.
